## User Experience (UX)

Mind Map:

- customer journey (landing page->search->basket->check)
- Use Cases (boat rental example)
- Personae <- market research
- interviews
- mockups
- metrics:
  - usage statistics
    * Google Analytics (location, time spent on site)
    * attention tracking
  - A/B testing
  - measure customer journey
- Nielsens usability heuristics
- design principles
  - accessibility & inclusive design
  - typography
  - iconography
  - colors

1. Write down 3 ideas for your website project

2. Create a persona (idealized user)
  - name
  - image
  - profession
  - hobbies
  - demographics (age, gender, nationality, family)
  - aspirations
  - wishes, goals
  - obstacles
  - frustrations

3. run interviews with the persona from another team
