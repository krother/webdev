About us

Our traditional boat-making company dates back to 1810, when our ancestors started folding origami boats and releasing them to the cherry blossom lake. Even though a lot has changed, some things don't: we are still making boats, and we are still a family business. Like in origami, we care about detail. Each boat is handcrafted.
While exploring the lake in our boats, you will feel light as a feather, as if you were in a paper boat.
